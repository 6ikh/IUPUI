Final Project Proposal

Project Description

I want to create an interactive HTML page for a gas station where customers can choose gas grade, checkout food items, and browse a local map and attractions.

Reason for Choosing This Topic

I chose this topic because my Father has a gas station and it would be nice to make a webpage for practice if I ever want to create one for him. It won't be necessarily the same but it will have many features I can use.

I decided to use a local gas station name from my home town called Kum & Go.

Background Knowledge

I have gone over some of my background regarding why I chose the topic but I have knowledge of HTML and Javascript from my other classes. Also I have knowledge of what services are provided in a real life gas station.

Intended Audience

The intended audience for this website is a customer looking to purchase gas or food. Also someone looking for directions. There are no limitations to who is or is not the intended audience
